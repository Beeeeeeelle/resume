  <section class="project-case__section">
    <h2>Study Design</h2>
    <ul class="project-case__list">
      <li>Tracks learners longitudinally rather than treating AI use as a one-time event or short-term exposure.</li>
      <li>Combines personal attributes, learning process indicators, and outcome-oriented evidence.</li>
      <li>Uses cross-population comparison to surface where AI-mediated learning may amplify or reduce inequity.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="project-case__section">
    <h2>What This Project Connects</h2>
    <div class="project-case__detail-grid">
      <div class="project-case__panel">
        <span class="project-case__panel-label">Personal Attributes</span>
        <p>How self-direction, motivation, confidence, and strategy use shape the way learners incorporate GenAI into ongoing study routines.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="project-case__panel">
        <span class="project-case__panel-label">Learning Process</span>

  </section>
