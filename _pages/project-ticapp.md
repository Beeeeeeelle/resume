  <div class="project-case__detail-grid">
    <div class="project-case__panel">
      <span class="project-case__panel-label">Problem Framing</span>
      <p>The project starts from a specific learner problem: many heritage Chinese learners can speak Chinese but struggle to retrieve, recognize, and type characters efficiently. Traditional instruction does not directly address that gap.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="project-case__panel">
      <span class="project-case__panel-label">Design Contribution</span>
      <p>TicApp reframes character learning around typing as a cognitively meaningful learning action, not just an input method, and uses app design to make that process teachable and trackable.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <section class="project-case__section">
    <h2>Project Highlights</h2>
    <ul class="project-case__list">
      <li>Frames Chinese character learning as a heavy cognitive load problem involving phonology, orthography, and semantics.</li>
      <li>Uses Pinyin-based typing to integrate sound, form, and meaning in a single learner action.</li>
      <li>Builds the system through design-based research with iterative feedback from authentic learner and teacher contexts.</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
