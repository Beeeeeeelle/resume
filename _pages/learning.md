## Design Approach

<div class="learning-intro">
  <p>Design and development work is where I translate research questions into usable learning tools, websites, course systems, and communication artifacts. Across product design, instructional design, visual storytelling, and evaluation, I focus on clarity, accessibility, and designs that people can actually use in educational settings.</p>
</div>

<div class="learning-overview" data-reveal-group data-reveal-step="72">
  <article class="learning-overview__card" data-reveal>
    <h3>Website Design</h3>
    <p>Create public-facing sites that organize research, tools, and practical resources into clear pathways for educators and learners.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="learning-overview__card" data-reveal>
    <h3>Learning Products</h3>
    <p>Design app, course, and resource experiences that translate learner needs into usable instructional flows.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="learning-overview__card" data-reveal>
    <h3>Media &amp; Visual Systems</h3>
    <p>Build newsletters, video studies, campaigns, and supporting visuals that strengthen communication and engagement.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="learning-overview__card" data-reveal>
    <h3>Evaluation</h3>
    <p>Use interviews, surveys, and design evidence to guide program decisions and improvement recommendations.</p>
  </article>
</div>

      <p class="learning-panel__eyebrow">Product design · Mobile learning · Design-based research</p>
      <h3>TicApp</h3>
      <p>TicApp is a mobile learning design project that turns a specific heritage Chinese typing challenge into a guided, app-based learning experience.</p>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Needs-to-interface translation:</strong> moved from learner pain points and contextual inquiry into a mobile-first flow with staged practice pathways.</li>
        <li><strong>Bilingual UX:</strong> designed microcopy and activity logic for learners navigating English and Mandarin support at the same time.</li>
        <li><strong>Research connection:</strong> the design work later became a Springer book chapter on design thinking for diverse learners.</li>
      </ul>
