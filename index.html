Designing Learning for the Age of AI

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Purdue University’s Learning Design and Technology program, co-advised by Dr. Victoria Lynn Lowell (Walker) and Dr. Marisa Exter . I earned my master’s degree in Instructional Systems Technology, Indiana University Bloomington , advised by Dr. Curtis J. Bonk . My research examines how learners and educators engage with generative AI — spanning systematic reviews, instrument development, and longitudinal mixed-methods studies. I have 641+ citations, an h-index of 12, and 13 first-author papers published in venues such as IEEE Transactions on Learning Technologies, Computers and Education: AI, and British Journal of Educational Technology.
