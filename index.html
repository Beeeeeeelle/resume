Design Toward the Future of Learning for ALL

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Purdue University’s Learning Design and Technology program, co-advised by Dr. Victoria Lynn Lowell (Walker) and Dr. Marisa Exter . I earned my master’s degree in Instructional Systems Technology, Indiana University Bloomington , advised by Dr. Curtis J. Bonk . After graduation, I worked as the product manager at HiLink in Chicago, as an Instructional & Research Manager in the Cyber Lab at IUPUI for a year, and as visiting faculty in the Chinese flagship program at Indiana University for three years, teaching language courses, overseeing and training tutors, and managing the tutoring program.

I focus on three research strands that connect theory, classroom design, and partnerships with multilingual learners.
