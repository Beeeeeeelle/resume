Designing Learning for All in the Age of AI

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Purdue University’s Learning Design and Technology program, co-advised by Dr. Victoria Lynn Lowell (Walker) and Dr. Marisa Exter . I earned my master’s degree in Instructional Systems Technology at Indiana University Bloomington , where I was advised by Dr. Curtis J. Bonk . My research focuses on how learners and educators engage with generative AI, particularly in self-directed learning, learner agency, and the design of AI-mediated learning environments. My work combines empirical research with design practice: I develop educational tools, have built my own learning app, and draw on three years of teaching and program experience at Indiana University Bloomington.
